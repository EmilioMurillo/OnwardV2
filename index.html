    WHAT IS ONWARD?

    Onward is a STEAM-based academy brought to your community by Boston Public School educators who see a need of opportunities for our youth.

    By participating in this academy, students will acquire collegiate level skill sets and work to actively create ways to build up their community.

    Additionally, trained participants will have greater access to employment opportunities from an earlier age.

    Our Vision

    Our vision of the Excellence for All STEAM School Network is to provide historically marginalized students from low-income, immigrant, and communities of color access to a high-quality STEAM education in a culturally responsive environment that centers their lived experiences.
